Steps 4 & 5 — Building the Engine / Full Speed Ahead

You've cleared high-interest debt, grabbed the employer match, and built your emergency fund. We're not picking stocks — we're buying a slice of the whole pie (index funds like the S&P 500 or target-date funds). The goal: 15% of gross income across these three vehicles.

The reliable all-rounder everyone picks first. Widest selection of funds — you choose your own course, unlike the employer-locked 401k menu. Not the flashiest contribution limit, but handles beautifully and rewards skilled drivers.

✅ Pros

Widest fund selection. Full control over investments.

⚠️ Cons

More hands-on to manage. Lower contribution cap.

⭐ Star at the finish line — pay taxes now, withdraw tax-free in retirement. Best if you expect a higher income later.
🍄 Mushroom boost now — tax deduction today lowers your taxable income. Best if you're in a higher bracket now than you expect later.

HSA
Health Savings Account
Speed
Flexibility
Tax Shield
Limit

The triple tax advantage on a bike — nimble, elegant, and quietly the best vehicle in the game. Requires an HDHP to unlock — a trickier course best suited for riders without expensive recurring health costs (younger, no chronic conditions). But for those who qualify, this shortcut is unbeatable.

✅ Pros

Only vehicle with three layers of tax shielding. Investable & grows.

⚠️ Cons

Requires HDHP. Best for younger/healthier riders.

🪙 Contributions lower your taxable income today — instant coin block reward.
📈 Zero taxes on interest or investment gains — your coins compound in peace.
💊 Withdraw tax-free for medical expenses — the finish line bonus no other vehicle gets. You never pay taxes on it.
Star Power: Triple Tax Exemption

The heavyweight bruiser with the biggest contribution limit on the track. Auto-deducted from your paycheck — set it and forget it. Handles like a tank (limited fund choices, employer-dependent), but raw power.

The employer match is a free item box every lap: you put in $1, boss puts in $1 — that's a 100% return before it even hits the market.

✅ Pros

Highest limit. Auto-deducted. Employer match = free coins.

⚠️ Cons

Limited fund menu. Early withdrawal = tax penalty (set aside ~20-25%).

⭐ Star power finish — tax-free withdrawals in retirement, with Bowser-sized contribution limits.
🍄 Mushroom boost now — reduces today's taxable income. Good if you're in a high bracket and expect to be lower later.
Star Power: Employer Match (Free Coins!)
